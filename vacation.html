Shibuya Scramble Crossing - Tokyo, Japan

The Shibuya Scramble Crossing in Tokyo, Japan, is one of the busiest and most famous intersections in the world. Known for its chaotic beauty, the crossing is a symbol of Tokyo’s vibrant energy. With hundreds of people crossing in all directions at once, it’s a must-see destination for anyone visiting the city. The surrounding area is filled with neon lights, shopping centers, and entertainment, making it a truly iconic experience.
